Who We Are

More Than a Football Academy

PTE Football Academy was born from a simple truth: talent without structure gets lost. Founded in Ibadan, Oyo State, we exist to give young Nigerians aged 6 to 16 what many never get — real coaching, real discipline, and a real future.

We are completely free of charge. No registration fees. No hidden costs. Just committed coaches, structured training, and young boys who deserve better than vibes and hope.

At PTE, football is the vehicle. Holistic development is the destination. We believe the same discipline that makes a great player builds a great person — and we coach both.
