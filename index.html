Who We Are

More Than a
Football Academy

PTE Football Academy Nigeria was built on a simple truth: talent without structure gets lost. Founded in Ibadan, we exist to give young Nigerians aged 6 to 16 what too many never get — proper coaching, real discipline, and a genuine future.

We are completely free of charge. No registration fees. No hidden costs. Just committed coaches, structured weekly training, and players who deserve more than just hope.

At PTE, football is the vehicle. Holistic development is the destination. The same discipline that builds a great player builds a great person.

Age Groups

Our Programs

Every age group has a dedicated structure and coaching focus tailored to where players are in their development.

U6
Grasshoppers

The youngest PTE family. We focus on fun, movement and first touches. Building love for the game from day one.

Ages 5 – 6
U8
Cubs

Coordination, basic skills and small-sided games. Players start understanding positions and teamwork.

Ages 7 – 8
U10
Falcons

Technical skills deepen. Ball control, dribbling, passing and quick decision-making become the core focus.

Ages 9 – 10
U12
Eagles

Tactical awareness builds. Players learn positional discipline, set pieces and competitive match preparation.

Ages 11 – 12
U14
Lions

High intensity sessions. Physical conditioning, formations, leadership and mental strength all built here.

Ages 13 – 14
U16
Elite Squad

The pathway to professional football. Structured exposure for the most dedicated and talented players at PTE.
